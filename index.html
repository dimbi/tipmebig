The most common tip amounts?

The most common tip amount is between 1~5 dollars. There are few counts of tip amount in the range of 20-100, but we treated these as outliers and didnâ€™t include them in the plot. Preliminary analysis of the data, based on the one-day (2013-08-04 Sunday) taxi data shown that the trips paid by cash usually have 0 tip in record, we only used the trips paid by credit card.

Fluctuations in higher fare amount.

The tip percentage follows a predictable pattern when the fare is below 50, while it fluctuate severely when the fare rises above 50. Other parameters might be a driving factor for this fluctuations. But overall, people gave higher percentage of tip when the trip fare is low, especially below 20, while lower percentage of tip when the fare on the high end.

Weekdays VS Weekends.

Based on that finding we then take one year data and see the difference of tipping behavior against different time of day, and contrasted weekend and weekdays behavior. In Weekdays, people paid less tips at 7-10am but more tips at 4-7pm. The result may reflect that people tend to tip more when getting off work at workdays.
